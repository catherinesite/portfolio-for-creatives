Bakkers

[With branding by Sol Yoon and Website by Jacob Chong]

Bakkers is a playful and colourful school supplies brand that focuses on using sustainable fabrics. For this group project, my contribution was in the making of the patterns for the fabrics that would be used in the products we sold. I mocked them up into backpacks to show what the product would look like.
